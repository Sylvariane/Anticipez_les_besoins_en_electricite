Energy Use & Green House Gas Emissions Simulator

City of Seattle

This website is here to help you to estimate the emission of GHG and the energy uses. This estimation is only available for the city of Seattle.
In order to predict your environmental impact, you need some informations:

  • The kind of Property;
  • The localisation (only the Neighborhood);
  • The year of construction;
  • The number of buildings on the Property;
  • The number of floors;
  • The GFA of all the property.
